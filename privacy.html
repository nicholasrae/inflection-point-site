Inflection Point

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 16, 2026 · Last Updated: February 16, 2026

Inflection Point is a life strategy and journaling app. This policy explains what information the app uses, how that information is handled, and what control you keep over your data.

1. Information the app uses

  • Journal entries and anti-vision exercises you create in the app
  • App preferences and settings
  • Device type, operating system, and app version
  • Usage, crash, and performance data used to improve stability
  • Purchase status and payment information processed by Apple

2. What we do not collect

  • Your journal content is not uploaded to our servers by default
  • No location data
  • No contacts, photos, or unrelated device data
  • No third-party browsing history

3. How your data is used

  • Your journal entries stay on your device unless you explicitly export or back them up
  • Usage and crash data help improve the app
  • Purchase data is used only to manage your lifetime unlock status

4. Storage and security

Your content is stored locally on your device. Optional iCloud backup, if enabled, is tied to your Apple account. Exported data leaves the device only when you choose to export it.

5. Third-party services

  • Apple handles payment processing and platform analytics
  • RevenueCat handles purchase status management

6. Your control

  • Export your data at any time
  • Delete entries or reset the app from within the app
  • Contact us if you have privacy questions
